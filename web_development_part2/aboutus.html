Bistré - how it all started

It all started with a passion for fresh food. Bistré has been the restaurant of choice for many locals in Hämeenlinna since 1986. We started with 20 seats, and extended two years later with an addition of 22 seats. Now, we are serving more than 80 customers daily. Our ultimate goal is to make our customers happy time after time.

All of our food is made in house from fresh & local ingredients with original recipes. In 2006, we were nominated as the best restaurant in Finland.

Our motto is: "Fresh is the best!"

Our story

Since a young age, Bistré's founder Pierre Bellini has been involved in the catering business. "My grandparents had a small French restaurant in Paris before WW2 and the escape to Italy. It got destroyed in the bombings. I've always wanted to keep the family tradition alive, and start my own business."

In 1986, Pierre Bellini came to Finland. He chose Visamäki in Hämeenlinna as the perfect location for his very own business; not too far from the centrum, but close to nature and it's organic ingredients.

"I have a wife and a family here. Life is good!" After all, the best dishes are made with Love.
